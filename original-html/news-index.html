Stay Informed of the Latest Retail News

design:retail is the leading resource for covering the retail news, awards, events, people and opinions that matter to today’s top retail industry executives. Covering new store openings, important industry happenings, manufacturer updates, industry surveys and more, design:retail helps store planners, visual merchandisers, retail designers, independent architects and contract designers stay informed in the fast-paced retail industry. 

By keeping up with new retail stores and events, planners and retail designers can find information and insight that is relevant to retailers, making them better prepared to develop successful ideas and environments for their clients. Manufacturers can stay on top of retail news and trends so that they are better equipped to deliver materials and products for high-impact retail environments. With this in mind, design:retail shares retail store design news and industry updates straightforward, concise format, making it easy for the retail industry to stay up to date. 

Retail Store Design that Matters

Sharing can’t-miss industry happenings from retailers, design and architecture firms and retail manufacturers, design:retail delivers relevant retail information daily. News coverage spans the luxury to discount markets, and includes updates from retail suppliers, offering everything from architectural materials to mannequin and décor. Design:retail’s news also recaps industry events and awards, and shares the views of some of the industry’s brightest, making design:retail an important, useful trend and information resource for retail designers and visual merchandisers looking to stay ahead of the competition.
